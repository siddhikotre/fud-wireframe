Fud is an AI-native "Action Marketplace for Content, Community, and Mentorship for Everyday Entrepreneurs and Small Business Owners.The worlds first social hustling community. The goal is to help everyday entrepreneurs and underserved small business owners take ACTION. Our hypothesis is Agentic AI is the best way to help people take ACTION today. In the past, we had focused on mentorship/coach, content, and community. The pivot is to make it even easier by giving regular people access to AI. We’re also going to NOT target side hustlers and gig workers, and shift to small business and micro-enterprises.


For Week 1 we are trying to create a foundation and here are the targets
1. Events/courses/workshops
We want the users to discover and participate in events/courses/workshops on the platform and apply with help of a single click, the user should be asked questions during onboarding and we are going to store that information and then use this information to apply to various events by a single click, if there is additional information which needs to be asked the user, the agent asks it once and then it get saved in database 

The events can be of two types: i. free ii.paid and they can be i. in-person ii. virtual

For paid workshops the user will pay using fud coins 

2. Fud coins
Users can buy Fud coins vis Stripe and then use these Fud coins to do various activities and attend events on Fud.

3. Affiliate Marketing 
AI agents recommend useful tools and the platform earns commission on referrals.
